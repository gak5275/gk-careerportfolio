Games I Have Created or Contributed to

Everyday

Play Everyday on itch.io

Everyday is a visual novel I created as my senior project for the Schreyer Honors College at Penn State. It follows a group of people at an indepent game company and the struggles they face while working in the modern video game industry.


The Insurance Game

Play The Insurance Game in your browser

In a collaboration with an insurance company, three college classmates and I created what we call The Insurance Game. It is a digital experience meant to teach users about insurance terms by using them in the context of a meeting with an agent.
