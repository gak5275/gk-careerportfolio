Stardew Valley Analysis

Spring 2023

Go to website

Two college classmates and I created a website on which we share our analysis of the video game Stardew Valley. (my favorite game!) We looked at the text in the game's code and found recurring themes in the dialogue. We also looked at which characters have the most lines of dialogue in specific locations throughout the game.
